About The Teacher

Meet Kiana Westra

Hi there! I'm a TEFL-certified English tutor who helps learners feel confident using English in real-life situations. If you want relaxed, supportive lessons where mistakes are welcomed and progress feels natural, you're in the right place.

What brought me to teaching English is my passion for linguistics, travel, and helping others. I've studied French, Spanish, Italian, German, Dutch, and Japanese, and I speak French and Spanish. As a language learner myself, I understand the challenges and how to make learning feel less intimidating and more enjoyable.

I love exploring the similarities and differences between languages, where they come from, and why they work the way they do. If you enjoy language history too, we might have a lot to talk about.
